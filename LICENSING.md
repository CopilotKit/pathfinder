## What you can do

- **Host Pathfinder for your own or your organization's docs.** Personal projects, open-source projects, company wikis, internal knowledge bases — all fine. That's the intended use case.

## What you can't do

- **Sell Pathfinder as a product or service.** You can't take Pathfinder, wrap it up, and sell it as your own information retrieval offering.

## Why this license?

We want Pathfinder to be freely usable by the developer community while preventing commercial competitors from reselling our work. The Elastic License 2.0 with our Additional Use Grant achieves both.
